Monthly costs paid regardles of usage

Premium

Routine services covered immediately

Copay

Things usually covered

  • Primary Care Doctor Visits
  • Specialist Visits (with higher copay)
  • Sometimes medications. Usually covered on low deductible plans and not on high. Copays usually greater for non-generics

Unexpected things covered after deductible

Things usually covered

  • Emergency Rooms
  • Hospital Stays
  • Tests, Diagnostics, X-Rays
  • Treaments
  • Sometime Medications

Before Deductible

Before deductible all things in this class are paid fully by patient.

Deductible

The deductible is the amount up until which you pay things in this class fully out of pocket.

After deductible

After the deductible insurance will kick in but you might still have to pay something.

Co-Insurance

After the deductible has been met and insurance kicked in you will often need to pay a co-insurance. co-insurance is usually a percentage of the fees and is often higher for out of network than in network if both are covered.

Out of Pocket Max

The absolute limit on how much you pay out of pocket for covered services. After this there is no more co-insurance.
